CARE GUIDE

How to get the best experience from your diffuser and candle.

Reed Diffuser Care

  • Insert all reeds and allow them to absorb the fragrance.
  • Flip reeds after 24 hours for stronger scent throw.
  • Flip once a week to refresh the aroma.
  • Avoid direct sunlight and strong airflow.

Candle Care

  • First burn: 2–3 hours to prevent tunneling.
  • Trim wick to 5mm before each burn.
  • Do not burn longer than 4 hours at a time.
  • Always place on a heat-resistant surface.
